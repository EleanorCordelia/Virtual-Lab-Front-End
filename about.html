About us

What is IELTS9 ?

IELTS9 is an AI-powered IELTS practice platform that allows users to improve their English skills anytime, anywhere, right from their mobile devices. With interactive exercises and instant scoring, IELTS9 provides detailed feedback to help you identify strengths and areas for improvement. This innovative platform ensures you have the tools to effectively prepare for the IELTS exam on the go and achieve your desired score without in-person coaching.

Our mission

Although English-speaking skills are essential today, learning a language can be challenging for many. As non-native speakers, we have faced years of struggle to gain adequate speaking confidence. This inspired us to create IELTS9, driven by the belief that it's possible to develop a learning experience that is accessible at any time and yields significant results in just a few months.

Through enhancing education, we strive to foster inclusive prosperity.
